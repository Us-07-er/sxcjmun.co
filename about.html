About SXCJ MUN

SXCJ Model United Nations (SXCJ MUN) is a premier platform that brings together young minds to engage in meaningful dialogue, debate, and diplomacy.

The conference aims to foster critical thinking, public speaking, research, and negotiation skills while promoting awareness of global issues.

At its core, SXCJ MUN celebrates diversity of thought and diplomacy.
